Privacy Policy

Welcome to Uni Papers. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you log in using Google OAuth, we collect and store certain information provided by Google, including :

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile picture URL

We also collect any academic materials, metadata (university, course, semester), or reports you voluntarily upload to the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to :

  • Authenticate your identity and manage your user account.
  • Display your profile details on your personal dashboard.
  • Attribute uploaded materials to your account.
  • Maintain security, prevent spam, and enforce our platform limit(s).

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using reputable cloud services and security practices intended to protect against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Data Retention and Account Deletion

We retain your profile data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your login credentials and personal account information will be removed or deactivated. Previously uploaded papers (unless deleted) may remain available on the platform and may continue displaying the username associated with the upload.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth for authentication. Google’s use of your information is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. We do not sell personally identifiable information. However, we may use or share anonymized, aggregated, or statistical information relating to platform usage, academic trends, and user activity for research, analytics, advertising, or commercial purposes.

6. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact at : unipapers.contact@gmail.com
